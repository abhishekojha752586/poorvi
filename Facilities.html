Poorvi Ambulance Facilities

Poorvi Ambulance Services, located in Delhi, New Delhi, is dedicated to providing high-
quality emergency medical transportation and comprehensive care to patients in need. With
a strong commitment to patient safety and comfort, Poorvi Ambulance Services offers a
range of advanced facilities and services to ensure the well-being of their patients during
critical moments.
Facilities and services provided by Poorvi Ambulance Services include:

1.Ventilator: Ambulance Services is equipped with state-of-the-art ventilators,
allowing them to provide respiratory support to patients who require assistance with
breathing. This facility is crucial for patients with respiratory
distress or other respiratory conditions.

2. Life Support Ambulance: The service offers life support ambulances equipped with
advanced medical equipment and trained medical professionals to provide immediate
and critical care during transportation. These ambulances are designed to handle
complex medical emergencies and ensure the stability and safety of patients.

3. ICU (Intensive Care Unit): Poorvi Ambulance Services provides fully-equipped ICU
ambulances, staffed with specialized medical personnel, to cater to patients requiring
intensive care during transportation. These ambulances are equipped with advanced
monitoring systems, life-saving medications, and specialized medical equipment to
ensure critical care is delivered promptly.

4. Medication: service ensures the availability of essential medications and emergency
drugs to address various medical conditions during transportation. This includes
administering necessary medications based on patient needs and monitoring their
response to treatment.

5. Smart TV for Entertainment: Poorvi Ambulance Services understands the importance of
keeping patients comfortable and engaged during long drives. They provide smart TVs in
their ambulances, allowing patients to enjoy entertainment, stay connected, or distract
themselves during transportation.

Luxury Sofa: To enhance patient comfort, Poorvi Ambulance Services offers luxury sofas
in their ambulances. These comfortable seating arrangements provide a relaxing
environment for patients throughout the journey.

With a focus on excellence and a commitment to delivering exceptional patient care, Poorvi
Ambulance Services has been dedicated to providing these facilities and services to their
patients. They continuously strive to improve their offerings based on feedback,
advancements in medical technology, and the evolving needs of their patients.
